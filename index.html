About UMWSC

UMWSC is a student-run organization which provides competitive soccer for women who want to continue playing at the college level without the commitment of being a varsity athlete.

The last two years, UMWSC has advanced to the national tournament in Round Rock, Texas, where we played UCLA, Vanderbilt, and Texas this past season.

UMWSC competes in the W-MASC Conference in NIRSA's Region 3 with other teams like Michigan State and Grand Valley. During the regional tournament, the team competes against other competitive schools such as University of Wisconsin and Ohio State.
